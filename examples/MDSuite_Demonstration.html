Systems¶

In this demonstration we have two of the same systems however sampled at different rates. This is so Green-Kubo analysis can be performed as well as Einstein and hopefully, will show their equivalence. Note the coordinates are unwrapped automatically by the program in the case of the Einstein approaches.
